# VenueSync User Manual
*Your Complete Guide to Event & Venue Booking Management*

---

## 1. Introduction to VenueSync
Welcome to VenueSync! VenueSync is an intuitive platform that brings Event Planners and Venue Owners together. Whether you are organizing a massive corporate event or managing a portfolio of premium banquet halls, VenueSync simplifies the entire process.

---

## 2. Getting Started
### 2.1 Registration & Login
1. Navigate to the **VenueSync** homepage.
2. Click on **Sign Up** or **Log In** in the top right corner.
3. Choose your role:
   - **Event Planner:** If you want to book venues and manage events.
   - **Venue Owner:** If you own properties and want to accept bookings.
4. Fill in your details and click **Create Account**.

---

## 3. Guide for Event Planners

### 3.1 Searching for a Venue
1. From your Dashboard, click on **Search Venues** in the left sidebar.
2. Use the filters at the top to narrow down by **Location, Capacity, Date,** or **Price**.
3. Browse the venue cards. Click **View Details** on any venue to see high-resolution photos, available amenities, pricing, and the venue's live availability calendar.

### 3.2 Requesting a Booking
1. On the Venue Details page, review the calendar to ensure your desired date is green (Available).
2. Click **Request Booking** on the right panel.
3. Fill out the Event Details form:
   - **Event Name & Type**
   - **Date & Time**
   - **Expected Guests** (Note: You cannot exceed the venue's maximum capacity).
   - **Contact Information & Special Requirements**
4. Click **Submit Booking Request**. The status will now be **Pending** until the Owner approves it.

### 3.3 Managing Payments & Invoices
1. Once an Owner approves your booking, the status changes to **Approved**.
2. Go to **My Bookings** and click **View Details** on the approved event.
3. Click **Proceed to Payment**.
4. You will see your Total Amount, Paid Amount, and Remaining Balance.
5. Enter the amount you wish to pay, select a payment method, and click **Record Payment**. You can also download a PDF Invoice from this page.

### 3.4 Managing Guests & QR Codes
1. In your Booking Details, click **Manage Guests**.
2. Click **Add Guest** to input names, emails, and plus-ones.
3. Use the **Generate All QR Codes** button to automatically create scannable entry tickets for your guests.
4. These QR codes can be scanned using the built-in **QR Scanner** on the day of the event to mark guests as "Checked In".

### 3.5 Budget Tracking & Scheduling
- **Budgeting:** Click **Budget & Resources** to allocate funds for Catering, Decor, etc., and track your real-time expenses against your budget.
- **Scheduling:** Click **Event Schedule** to build a minute-by-minute run-of-show. You can export this schedule as a PDF to share with vendors.

## 4. Guide for Venue Owners

### 4.1 Managing Your Venues
1. From your Dashboard, go to **Manage Venues**.
2. Here, you will see a list of all your properties.
3. You can click the **Add New Venue** button to list a new property by providing its name, location, capacity, price, and amenities.

### 4.2 Viewing the Availability Calendar
1. In the **Manage Venues** page, click the **Calendar** button next to any of your active venues.
2. The dynamic Availability Calendar will appear at the bottom.
   - **Green:** Available
   - **Yellow:** Pending Booking Request
   - **Red:** Confirmed/Approved Booking
3. Hover over any Yellow or Red date to see the exact Event Name and Status.

### 4.3 Approving Bookings
1. Navigate to your **Dashboard**. 
2. Under "Recent Bookings", look for any requests with a **Pending** status.
3. Review the requested dates and guest counts.
4. Change the status to **Approved** to allow the planner to proceed with payments, or **Rejected** if you cannot accommodate them.

### 4.4 Tracking Revenue
Your main **Dashboard** gives you a financial overview:
- **Total Revenue:** Shows the total value of all approved bookings.
- **Pending Payments:** Shows money that is still owed by planners.
- **Completed Events:** A tally of successful events hosted at your properties.

## 5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: What happens if I try to invite more guests than the venue capacity?**
A: VenueSync has built-in safety limits. The system will alert you and prevent the booking or guest list from exceeding the strict limits set by the Venue Owner.

**Q: Can I pay more than the invoice balance?**
A: No, the payment system ensures that you cannot accidentally overpay the remaining balance on any booking.

**Q: How do I scan QR codes?**
A: Navigate to the **QR Scanner** tool from the sidebar on the day of the event. Use a webcam or mobile camera to scan guest tickets for instant check-in.
